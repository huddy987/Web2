Spotlighted Project Name: Web2

Project information:

This website, named Web2, was started in the Summer of 2018, and completed in the Fall of 2018. Web2 was my initiative to learn to make a functional and presentable website from scratch. The vision for this website was to be a place for me to share my projects easily. Using HTML, CSS, and JS, along with help from online tutorials, I managed to achieve just that.

A Raspberry Pi 3 B+ was used to host the website due to it's low cost, small size, low power usage, and relatively good computing power. Apache was chosen as the HTTP server due to it's simplicity to use. Below shows a size comparison of my Raspberry Pi and a credit card.
